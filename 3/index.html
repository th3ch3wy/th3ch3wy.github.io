Project 3 — Imaging Warping and Mosaicing

Instead of trusting my shaky hand without a tripod, I decided to take my “photos” in Valorant — my “favorite” game — using the observer mode and walking around the map. I also had to crop out around 45% of the outer edge for the photos because the Valorant player POV has a wide FOV and fisheye distortion.
